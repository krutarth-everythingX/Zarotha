# Assumptions

## Business Assumptions

- Zarokha sells through offline conversations after inquiry rather than direct online checkout.
- The main conversion goal is qualified lead generation, not self-service transaction completion.
- The business can provide timely follow-up on inquiries from the CMS and WhatsApp.

## Content Assumptions

- Final brand story, About content, and Craftsmanship copy are not yet supplied and remain client dependencies.
- Product names, descriptions, dimensions, materials, finishes, categories, and collection assignments will be supplied or curated by the client.
- Privacy Policy and Terms and Conditions content require client or legal approval before launch.
- No customer testimonials, metrics, awards, or certifications will be published unless explicitly supplied and verified by the client.

## Image Assumptions

- High-quality product photography is required for launch and is a client dependency.
- Featured images and galleries need sufficient resolution for large-display product viewing.
- Alt-text context may require editorial input from the content team unless the media workflow captures it directly later.

## Contact And Inquiry Assumptions

- The client will provide approved phone, email, WhatsApp number, and contact-handling ownership.
- Inquiry recipients, assignment rules, response expectations, and archival rules are not yet defined and remain operational dependencies.
- Consent language and required form disclaimers depend on final legal guidance.

## WhatsApp Assumptions

- WhatsApp is a primary conversion channel and should open with an approved business number.
- Prefilled messages may be used, but they should stay editable and factual.

## Google Maps Assumptions

- A map or location reference on the Contact page is optional and depends on whether the client wants a public address shown.
- If a map is used later, it should rely on an approved stable embed or API approach and not expose unnecessary privacy risk.

## Technical Assumptions

- The implementation will use the latest stable Laravel version available when coding begins.
- The CMS will use React, Inertia.js, TypeScript, and the supplied admin UI kit.
- MySQL will be the primary database.

## Scope Assumptions

- Best-selling products are editorially flagged unless real sales integration is added later.
- Latest products are controlled by publish date and or an editorial flag.
- Instagram or gallery integration is optional and should be implemented only if a stable approved source is available.

## Open Dependencies Requiring Client Input

- Approved logo assets and brand guidelines.
- Final product catalogue dataset for launch.
- Collection names, definitions, and curation rules.
- Category taxonomy and filter priorities.
- Approved contact details and WhatsApp number.
- Privacy Policy and Terms content.
- Address visibility decision and map preference.
- Inquiry ownership, reply SLA expectations, and internal workflow preferences.
- Whether customization, made-to-order messaging, or trade inquiries should be highlighted publicly.
