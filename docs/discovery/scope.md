# Scope

## In Scope for Launch

### Public website pages

- Home
- Collections
- Collection Details
- Products
- Product Details
- About Us
- Our Craftsmanship
- Contact
- Privacy Policy
- Terms and Conditions
- 404

### Homepage sections

- Hero banner or slider
- Featured collections
- Best-selling products
- About Zarokha
- Why choose us
- Craftsmanship process
- Latest products
- Customer inquiry call to action
- Optional Instagram or gallery section
- Footer

### Catalogue and inquiry capabilities

- Browse products by collection and category.
- Search or filter products by relevant attributes.
- View product specifications, imagery, related products, and inquiry options.
- Submit product-linked inquiries and general contact inquiries.
- Start WhatsApp contact from appropriate surfaces.

### CMS modules

- Dashboard
- Products
- Categories
- Collections
- Product galleries
- Media library
- Homepage content
- Banners
- About page
- Craftsmanship page
- Contact information
- Inquiries
- SEO
- Users and roles
- Settings
- Activity history where appropriate

## Explicitly Excluded from Current Scope

- Cart
- Checkout
- Online payment
- Order management
- Shipping workflows
- Inventory management
- Customer account area
- Public user registration
- Wishlists
- Coupon or promotion engine
- Stock alerts
- Fabricated testimonials, metrics, awards, or certifications

## Scope Clarifications Locked in Stage 1

- No cart.
- No checkout.
- No online payment.
- No order management.
- No inventory management.
- No customer account area.
- No public user registration.
- No invented testimonials or business statistics.
- The site is a catalogue and inquiry platform, not ecommerce.
- Collections are curated product groups and categories are product taxonomy.
- "Best selling" is an editorial CMS flag unless real sales data is integrated later.
- "Latest" is controlled through publication date and or a CMS flag.
- Instagram integration is optional and should use a stable approved source, not an unauthorized scraper.
- Product detail pages must prioritize inquiry conversion over transactional behavior.

## Launch-Scope Functional Boundaries

- Product information is descriptive and inquiry-oriented.
- Inquiry submission is stored internally for staff follow-up.
- Phone, email, and WhatsApp are conversion channels, not authenticated user features.
- SEO and content management are part of launch scope because discoverability is a core business need.

## Future Scope Candidates

- Ecommerce transaction flow.
- Inventory and stock visibility.
- Customer accounts and saved favorites.
- Blog, lookbook, case studies, or editorial stories.
- Multi-language support.
- Advanced analytics dashboards.
- CRM, ERP, PIM, or marketing automation integrations.

## Out of Scope for Stage 1 Work

- Laravel project creation.
- Database design implementation.
- UI implementation.
- Admin UI kit modifications.
- Any application code, migrations, components, CSS, or JavaScript.
