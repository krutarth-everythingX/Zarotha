# Project Brief

## Overview

Zarokha Wooden Arts is a Vadodara-based handcrafted wooden decor brand that needs a premium catalogue website and CMS. The website's role is to present the brand credibly, help visitors discover products quickly, and convert interest into inquiries through forms, phone, email, and WhatsApp.

## Business Objective

- Present Zarokha as a premium handcrafted decor brand.
- Increase qualified product and general-contact inquiries.
- Make product discovery easy across collections, categories, and editorial homepage highlights.
- Give internal staff a manageable CMS for product, imagery, homepage, SEO, and inquiry operations.

## Product Model

- Website type: inquiry-based product catalogue.
- Transaction model: offline sales and consultation after inquiry.
- Explicit non-scope: cart, checkout, payment, shipping, inventory, customer accounts, and public registration.

## Primary Conversion Actions

- Submit a product inquiry from a product detail page.
- Initiate a WhatsApp conversation from product or contact surfaces.
- Submit a general contact inquiry.
- Call or email the business from prominent contact calls to action.

## Target Visitors

- Homeowners seeking handcrafted premium decor pieces.
- Interior designers sourcing decorative products for projects.
- Architects, stylists, and boutique hospitality buyers seeking curated decor.
- Returning visitors comparing finishes, dimensions, and styles before contacting the brand.

## Admin User Types

- Super admin: full CMS access, settings, users, SEO, and inquiry oversight.
- Content manager: manages products, collections, galleries, homepage content, and static pages.
- Inquiry manager: reviews, replies to, exports, assigns, and archives inquiries.
- SEO or marketing editor: updates metadata, social previews, banners, and editorial highlights.

## Public Website Responsibilities

- Communicate the brand story and handcrafted positioning.
- Provide clear access to collections, categories, and products.
- Support search, filtering, browsing, and related-product discovery.
- Present product imagery, dimensions, material, finish, and descriptions clearly.
- Offer reliable inquiry and WhatsApp contact paths on key pages.
- Support SEO, accessibility, performance, and share previews.

## CMS Responsibilities

- Manage catalogue structure through categories, collections, products, and galleries.
- Manage homepage modules, banners, About content, Craftsmanship content, and contact information.
- Store and manage inquiries with status workflow and internal notes.
- Manage SEO metadata and canonical behavior.
- Manage users, permissions, and activity history where appropriate.

## Launch Scope Summary

- Public pages defined in the master rules.
- CMS modules defined in the master rules.
- Searchable, filterable catalogue and product inquiry workflow.
- WhatsApp, phone, email, and contact form conversion paths.
- Editorial homepage sections including featured, best-selling, latest, and brand-story blocks.

## Future Scope Summary

- Ecommerce capabilities if later approved.
- Multilingual content.
- Blog or editorial publishing.
- CRM integrations, analytics dashboards, or ERP/PIM integrations.
- Deeper media workflows such as AI-assisted tagging or DAM synchronization.

## Success Metrics

- Increase in qualified monthly inquiries.
- Product inquiry completion rate from product detail pages.
- Contact-form completion rate.
- WhatsApp click-through rate from product and contact pages.
- Search and filter engagement on catalogue pages.
- Product-detail page engagement, especially gallery interaction.
- CMS publishing efficiency for products and homepage updates.

## Constraints

- No application code or implementation in Stage 1.
- Admin UI kit must be reused later without redesign.
- Unknown business facts must remain marked as dependencies, not invented content.
