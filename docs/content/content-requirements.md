# Content Requirements

## Content Principles

- Publish only client-approved factual content.
- Do not invent testimonials, statistics, certifications, addresses, or legal claims.
- Treat imagery as core content, not decoration.
- Keep copy aligned with a premium handcrafted brand voice once supplied.

## Public Page Content Matrix

| Page | Required Content | Required Assets | Dependency Status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Home | Hero copy, supporting intro, featured collections, best-selling selection, latest products, About summary, Why choose us copy, craftsmanship summary, inquiry CTA, optional Instagram or gallery content, footer contact details | Hero images, collection images, product card images, brand visuals | Pending client content |
| Collections | Collection names, short descriptions, ordering | Collection cover images | Pending client content |
| Collection Details | Collection title, summary or story, associated products | Collection hero or cover image | Pending client content |
| Products | Product listing data, filter labels, search-relevant naming | Product card images | Pending client content |
| Product Details | Name, short description, full description, dimensions, material, finish, category, collection links where applicable, SEO fields, inquiry copy | Featured image, gallery images, social preview image when needed | Pending client content |
| About Us | Brand story, values, workshop or origin information that is verified | Brand or workshop photography if available | Pending client content |
| Our Craftsmanship | Process steps, making approach, materials story, care or quality context if approved | Process images or workshop images | Pending client content |
| Contact | Phone, email, WhatsApp number, address decision, form helper copy, consent text, map decision | Optional map asset or embed inputs | Pending client and legal input |
| Privacy Policy | Approved privacy copy | None unless legal branding requires it | Pending legal approval |
| Terms and Conditions | Approved terms copy | None unless legal branding requires it | Pending legal approval |
| 404 | Recovery heading, short helper text, links to key pages | Optional lightweight image or illustration | Can be authored internally with client review |

## Product Content Requirements

- Product name
- Slug source or slug-generation policy
- Category assignment
- Collection assignment where applicable
- Short description
- Full description
- Dimensions
- Material
- Finish
- Featured image
- Gallery images
- Meta title
- Meta description
- Open Graph image when needed
- Editorial flags for featured, best-selling, and latest behavior
- Publication status and date

## Taxonomy And Editorial Dependencies

- Confirm final category taxonomy.
- Confirm collection definitions and whether products can appear in multiple collections.
- Confirm the editorial rules for featured, best-selling, and latest labels.
- Confirm related-product logic expectations for launch.

## Contact And Inquiry Content Dependencies

- Approved phone number
- Approved email address
- Approved WhatsApp number
- Inquiry success message copy
- Validation and helper text copy
- Consent language and privacy references
- Internal subject-routing conventions if desired

## Legal Content Dependencies

- Privacy Policy text approved by the client or legal reviewer
- Terms and Conditions text approved by the client or legal reviewer
- Consent checkbox copy if required
- Any business-disclosure requirements for the Contact page

## Image Dependencies

- Product image library for launch products
- Collection cover images
- Homepage hero images or slides
- About and Craftsmanship imagery if those pages should include photography
- Social preview image strategy for key shareable pages

## Google Maps Dependency

- Decision on whether a public address should be shown
- Decision on whether a map embed is desired
- Approved address text if public display is allowed

## Operational Content Dependencies

- Inquiry owner names or roles for internal handling
- Response tone guidance for lead follow-up
- Any approved FAQ-style messaging for common inquiries
