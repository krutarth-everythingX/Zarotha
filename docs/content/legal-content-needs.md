# Legal Content Needs

## Legal Content Scope

- Privacy Policy
- Terms and Conditions
- Inquiry-form consent and disclosure copy if required
- Contact-page business disclosure details if the client wants public address or regulatory text displayed

## Privacy Policy Needs

- Effective date
- Identity of the business entity controlling the website data
- Contact method for privacy-related questions
- What inquiry-form data is collected
- Why the data is collected
- How inquiry data is used for follow-up
- Whether referrer, UTM, or abuse-prevention metadata is stored
- Whether data is shared with service providers
- Retention approach if the client has one
- Visitor rights or contact route if applicable under the chosen legal guidance
- Cookie or analytics disclosures if later relevant

## Terms And Conditions Needs

- Effective date
- Scope of website use
- Catalogue and inquiry nature of the website
- Accuracy and availability disclaimer if approved by legal review
- Intellectual property statement for site content and imagery
- Inquiry-submission expectations and non-contractual nature of catalogue browsing
- Limitation or governing-law language if required by legal review
- Contact route for terms-related questions

## Inquiry Form Legal Needs

- Consent checkbox copy if legally or operationally required
- Short privacy disclosure near the submit action if required
- Link targets for Privacy Policy and Terms and Conditions
- Any required marketing-consent distinction if later introduced

## Contact Page Legal Needs

- Decision on whether a public address should be shown
- Decision on whether any map embed should be used later
- Approved business name formatting
- Approved contact channels for public display

## Legal Publication Rules

- Do not invent legal text.
- Do not publish legal pages without approved content.
- Do not expose address or map details without explicit approval.
- Keep Google Maps out of implementation scope unless approved later.
