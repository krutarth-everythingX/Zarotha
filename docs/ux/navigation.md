# Navigation

## Navigation Goals

- Keep discovery simple, calm, and premium.
- Make Products the primary browse destination.
- Keep inquiry actions visible without introducing ecommerce behavior.
- Maintain predictable keyboard, mobile, and breadcrumb behavior.

## Desktop Header

- Primary navigation items: Home, Products, About Us, Our Craftsmanship, Contact.
- Utility actions may include WhatsApp and a direct inquiry shortcut when configured.
- Brand mark links to Home.
- Product search remains within the Products experience rather than becoming a separate nav item.

## Mobile Menu

- Mirrors the desktop information architecture.
- Keeps contact shortcuts visible without deep nesting.
- Uses accessible open and close controls with focus management and Escape support.

## Active States

- Current section should be visually indicated without relying on color alone.
- Product detail pages map to the Products section.
- Fixed pages map to their own top-level items.

## Footer Navigation

- Includes Products, About Us, Our Craftsmanship, Contact, Privacy Policy, and Terms and Conditions.
- May include product-category shortcuts when useful.
- Uses only approved business contact details.

## Breadcrumbs

- Required on product detail pages.
- Optional on fixed informational pages when they improve clarity.
- Not required on the homepage.

## Governance Rules

- No Collections navigation item remains in launch scope.
- No navigation item may imply cart, checkout, ordering, payment, or account flows.
- Optional Instagram or map links remain excluded unless separately approved.
