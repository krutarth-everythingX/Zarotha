# Text Wireframes

## Public Pages

### Home

1. Header with brand, navigation, and contact shortcut
2. Hero with image, headline, supporting copy, and primary CTA
3. Featured categories or products
4. About editorial band
5. Craftsmanship preview
6. Additional featured products or gallery strip when configured
7. Inquiry CTA
8. Footer

### Products Index

1. Header
2. Page title and optional intro
3. Search, category filter, and sort controls
4. Active filter summary
5. Product grid
6. Empty-state block when needed
7. Pagination
8. Footer

### Product Detail

1. Header
2. Breadcrumbs
3. Product gallery and primary image
4. Product title, category, short summary, inquiry actions
5. Full description
6. Specification list
7. Related products
8. Footer

### About Us

1. Header
2. Page title and intro
3. Editorial story blocks
4. Supporting image block if supplied
5. CTA to products or contact
6. Footer

### Our Craftsmanship

1. Header
2. Page title and intro
3. Ordered craftsmanship steps or sections
4. Supporting imagery
5. CTA to products
6. Footer

### Contact

1. Header
2. Page title and short intro
3. Contact shortcuts
4. Contact form
5. Privacy or consent helper text
6. Footer

### Privacy Policy

1. Header
2. Page title and effective date
3. Long-form legal text with section headings
4. Footer

### Terms And Conditions

1. Header
2. Page title and effective date
3. Long-form legal text with section headings
4. Footer

### 404

1. Header
2. Error heading and calm explanation
3. Recovery links to Home, Products, and Contact
4. Footer
